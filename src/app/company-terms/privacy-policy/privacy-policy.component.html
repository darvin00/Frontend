Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when:

  • Registering an account.
  • Placing an order.
  • Contacting customer support.

This may include your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and payment information. Additionally, we automatically collect data, such as your browser type, IP address, and browsing behavior, to improve user experience and website security.

2. Use of Your Information

Your personal information is used to:

  • Process orders and manage your account.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Personalize your shopping experience with tailored recommendations.
  • Notify you about updates, promotions, and events.
  • Enhance website functionality and security.

3. Banking and Payment Information

We take stringent measures to safeguard your financial data:

  • Payment information is transmitted securely using encryption.
  • We do not store your credit or debit card details on our servers.
  • Banking information is used solely for transaction processing and is not shared with third parties, except as required by law.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site, helping us recognize your preferences and tailor the website to better suit your needs.
